About Me:

I'm Khalid,


Hello! I'm a passionate and dedicated full-stack web developer with a strong academic foundation in mathematics and a growing portfolio of web-based projects. My journey began in the early 1990s in Oyo State, Nigeria, where I was born and raised. I laid the groundwork for my education in primary school from 1998 to 2003, followed immediately by secondary education, which I completed in 2009. These formative years instilled in me a deep appreciation for learning and problem-solving—skills that would later become central to my work as a developer. In pursuit of academic excellence, I enrolled at Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH), where I earned a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech Hons) degree in Pure and Applied Mathematics in 2019. My studies helped me develop a logical, analytical mindset and a deep understanding of complex systems—qualities that naturally aligned with the world of software development. Driven by curiosity and a desire to create, I officially began my journey into tech in 2023, diving into the world of full-stack web development. Over the course of a year, I immersed myself in both front-end and back-end technologies, earning a professional certificate in full-stack website development. This experience gave me hands-on knowledge of tools and languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, and databases—empowering me to build responsive, scalable, and user-centric web applications. Today, I combine my mathematical background with my technical skill set to design and develop digital solutions that are not only functional but also elegant. I’m constantly learning, evolving, and seeking new challenges that push my capabilities further. Whether it's building intuitive user interfaces or developing robust back-end systems, I approach each project with the same dedication and drive to deliver high-quality results.
